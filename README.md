### Note
The following research papers are mainly derived from over five years of observation in financial markets. While Quantitative models are employed, I didn't lean on historical data as the primary way to forecast anything. I'm guided by the spirit of the Lucas Critique. Markets aren't static — people adapt, policies shift, and behavior changes in ways that make old statistical relationships unreliable. Therefore, I put more weight on qualitative judgment and understanding the specific context of each situation, rather than trying to force everything into purely data-driven models.
